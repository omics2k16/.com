Omics

Paper Presentation

“Ideas not coupled with action never become bigger than the brain cells they occupied.”

Arnold Glasgow-

"There are numerous people around the world, from all walks of life, counting on young and aspiring researchers to come up with solutions that will help solve their not-so-pleasant puzzles quicker. And what can we do? We are here to bring about this change through paper presentations.".

Rules

1)2 per team.

2)Abstract not exceeding 250 words should be sent to the omics2k16@gmail.com with keywords, Mentor’s name, students name, e-mail id, college name.

3)Font style: Times New Roman

4)Font Size: Heading-14, Content-12, Line spacing-1.5.

Topics for paper/poster:

1)Genetic Engineering

2)Cancer Biology

3)Bioinformatics and Molecular Modelling

4)Bio Process and Food Process engineering

5)Nanotechnology

6)Therapeutic and Diagnostics

(And relevant areas of Biotechnology)
